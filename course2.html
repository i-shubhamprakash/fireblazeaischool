Commitment 24 Hours

About Course

This program of Fireblaze AI School focuses on building candidates foundation in the world of Data. In this program we assume that the candidate has no prior coding experience and train him from basis of programming in Python to performing Exploratory Data Analysis. This would help the participants go from a real world business problem to a first cut, working and deployable Data solution to the problem.

Objectives

  • This course has No prerequisites as it is the foundation course in the world of Data.
  • Students will develop relevant programming abilities in Python.
  • Students will apply data science concepts and methods to solve problems in real-world contexts and will communicate these solutions effectively

Target Audience

  • Professionals/Students from the technical stream (B.E, BTEC, BCA, MCA, BBA, POLY)
  • Students who are pursuing the graduation in any technical stream.
  • Pass out graduate or post graduate students in any technical stream.
  • Open-minded technology lovers those who wanted to explore IT profession.
